Our Legacy

Preserving Traditional Silver Artistry

SaiVedha began with a singular focus: to preserve the dying arts of traditional Indian silversmithing. For over three decades, we have partnered with master artisans from across regions to create certified, pure silver articles.

From heavy pooja items and temple collections to our signature antique silverware and museum-style urlis, we merge classic heritage styling with modern quality certifications. Each design undergoes rigorous hallmarking to ensure transparency and trustworthiness.
